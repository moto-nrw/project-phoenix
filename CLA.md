# Contributor License Agreement

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Project Phoenix ("Project"), owned and managed by moto-nrw ("Organization"). This Contributor License Agreement ("Agreement") documents the rights granted by contributors to the Organization.

## Why a CLA?

This CLA helps protect both you as a contributor and the Project. It ensures:
- You have the right to make your contribution
- The Project can legally use and distribute your contribution
- The Project can be relicensed if needed (e.g., for compatibility with other licenses)

## How to Sign

By submitting a pull request to this repository, you agree to the terms of this CLA. Your Git commit metadata (name and email) serves as your electronic signature.

For corporate contributions (on behalf of your employer), please have an authorized representative email the maintainers.
